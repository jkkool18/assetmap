NYC Community Assets Map

Whether you are looking for programs for your students, resources to share with families and/or opportunities to volunteer in your neighborhood, this community assets list is a one-stop shop for local searches.

This list features over 300 organizations in New York City devoted to health, education, arts, legal services, sports, LGBTQ communities, immigration, the environment, affordable housing and much more. We know that this list is just getting started, so if you are looking for something but not finding it or have assets to add, submit your ideas via the links at the bottom.
